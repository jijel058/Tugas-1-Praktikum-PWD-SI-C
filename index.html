Macarons Trivia

When did macaron first appear in public?

The macaron, in its original form as a single-shell almond cookie, first gained significant public notoriety in France in 1792. (Source: Nikkolette's Macarons)

Did you know where macarons come from?

Macarons actually originated in Italy and were later introduced to France in the 16th century by Catherine de Medici’s pastry chefs.

Why are macarons so colorful?

Macarons are known for their bright pastel colors because each color usually represents a different flavor such as strawberry, chocolate, matcha, or vanilla.

Why are macarons difficult to make?

Macarons require precise measurements, careful mixing techniques, and perfect baking temperatures. Even small mistakes can cause the shells to crack or collapse.
